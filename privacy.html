Privacy Policy

At Web Pixel Arcade, accessible from https://webpixelarcade.github.io, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Web Pixel Arcade and how we use it.

Log Files

Web Pixel Arcade follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, Web Pixel Arcade uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

Third-Party Privacy Policies

Our advertising partners (such as Adsterra) use technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons that are used in their respective advertisements and links that appear on Web Pixel Arcade. These technologies automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Please note that Web Pixel Arcade has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

Children's Information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity. Web Pixel Arcade does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13.
